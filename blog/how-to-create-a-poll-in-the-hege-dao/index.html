How To Create a Poll in the Hege DAO

Learn how to create a poll proposal for getting the opinion from members in the Hege DAO.

by Day

Info / October 21, 2025

This post is part of a series of posts about the Hege DAO, see How To Use the Hege DAO for an introduction and overview.

How to create a poll

NOTE: To create a poll, you must first be a member of the Hege DAO. If you are not already a member, please follow the instructions in How to Become a Member of the Hege DAO, and then come back here.

This article will show how to create a poll proposal for getting the opinion from member in the Hege DAO. (If you want to make a spending proposal to spend funds from the DAO treasury, see How to Create a Spending Request instead.)

When you are a member of the Hege DAO, here is how to create a poll proposal

  1. Go to the Hege DAO page on Realms. Connect your wallet. It should look something like this. You can see how many $HEGE tokens you have deposited in the upper right corner, right next to the banner. (NOTE: You must have at least 10,000 $HEGE deposited to make a proposal. See How to Deposit and Withdraw to learn how to deposit tokens.)
  1. Click “Create Proposal”.
  1. Fill in the title and description of your proposal.
  1. Select the Vote Type to “Non-Executable”. Then click the “Next” button.
  1. Add your choices for the poll. Add up to a total of 10 choices by clicking “Add Option” below the choice fields.
  1. Chose whether you want your poll to be single or multiple selection. Then click the “Next” button.
  1. Review the information that you filled in. Make sure everything is correct.
  1. Select “No” on vote by multi-sig. Then click “Create Proposal”.
  1. Confirm the transaction in your Solana wallet.
  1. Your proposal is now created! If a share dialog is shown, close it.
  1. You now see a page showing information about your proposal. Go back to the main page of the Hege DAO by clicking on “Hege DAO” in the upper left corner of the page.
  1. Back at the main page, you can now see your new proposal in the list of proposals.
  1. That’s it! Your proposal is created and open for voting. Now go share it with the Hege community! And don’t forget to vote on your proposal. See How to Vote in the Hege DAO if you need help with that.
